Fantastic Johnson Waffles

They are so tasty its mind blowing!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 Cup of flour
  • 3 Tablespoons of sugar (more if desired)
  • 1 Teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 Cup of water
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of vanilla
  • 2 Tablespoons of cooking oil
  • (Optional) Chocolate Chips

Preparation

First get your waffle maker plugged in and heating up. Or a griddle if you want to turn this into pandcakes.

Next mix your dry and wet ingredients separately. Preferably, mix your wet ingredients in a larger sized bowl as you will be adding the dry into the wet.

If you are making waffles mix the dry and wet ingredients together untill the mixture is smooth.
If you are going for pandcakes try to mix the dough less so that it stays a bit lumpy.

I find that most 8 inch waffle makers like just over 1/2 cup of batter, or 1 and a half of the 1/3 cup sized scoops. (If you don't want to wash extra dishes.)

Makes 2 of the 8 inch waffles, or 4 of the 1/3 cup sized pandcakes.
Increase batter volume as needed. 1-2 eggs worth of batter feeds a hungry man.

Enjoy!
