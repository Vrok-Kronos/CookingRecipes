Pizza Dough

Classic Italian pizza dough.

Ingredients

Note: This recipe uses baker's percentages as a ratio of the total flour to be used.

  • Flour
  • 60% water
  • 2% salt
  • 1% yeast
  • A little olive oil

Optional Ingredients

  • 2% sugar for sweeter dough
  • Garlic powder or other spices for some extra flavor
  • A dash of corn flour for non-stick performance

Preparation

Prof the yeast by adding it to the 60% water at room temperature. If you are using sugar mix it into the water first.

Mix the salt into to flour before adding to the water and yeast so you dont sterilize your yeast.

Knead into appropriate sized dough balls. If you are going to raise the dough on the counter, brush some olive oil on the surface to keep it from drying out.

Optional: Slow raised dough tastes better. After kneading place in a cold refrigerator (1-2*C) for 24-48 hours. (If your making pizzas on the weekend mix your dough thursday or friday afternoon.)
