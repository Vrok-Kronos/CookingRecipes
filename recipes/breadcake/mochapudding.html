Mocha Pudding

Its So Chocolaty!

Ingredients (Batter)

  • 1/3 Cup (80ml) of shortening
  • 2/3 Cup (160ml) of white sugar
  • 1 Cup (240ml) of flour
  • 1 & 1/2 Teaspoons (7.5ml) of baking powder
  • 1/4 Teaspoon (1.25ml) of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 Teaspoon (2.5ml) vanilla
  • 1/3 Cup (80ml) of milk

Ingredients (Sauce)

  • 1/4 Cup (60ml) of cocoa powder
  • 1/2 Cup (120ml) of white sugar
  • 1/2 Cup (120ml) of brown sugar
  • 2 Cups (480ml) of brewed coffee

Preparation

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees (180 Celsius).

Mix the shortening, sugar, vanilla, and egg(s) together thoroughly in a large bowl. Mix the flour, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl.

Add the dry ingredients to the batter and stir in thoroughly. Disolve the cocoa powder and sugars into freshly brewed coffee.

Place the batter in an appropriately sized baking pan. Pour sauce over the batter. Place pan onto a middle oven rack and cook for 15-20 minutes.

Serve warm with fresh wipping cream (wipped).

Enjoy!
