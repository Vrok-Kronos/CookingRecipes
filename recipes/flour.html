Gluten Free Flour

This flour is gluten free and you can hardly tell the difference.

You will need the following:

  • Rice Flour
  • Corn Flour
  • Tapioca(or Yuca) Flour

An ideal mix is of equal parts. I.e. 500g of each, for a total of 1.5kg.
Mix in a large container for storage and quick use.

You will need to add about 1/4 teaspoon of xanthun gum powder per 1 cup of flour in a recipe.
