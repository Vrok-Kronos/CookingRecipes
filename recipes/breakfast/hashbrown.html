Classic Hashbrowns

Potato-y-Goodness.

Ingredients

  • Potato(es)
  • Salt

Optional Ingredients

  • Butter or Oil
  • Pepper
  • Diced Onion
  • Diced green/red peppers
  • Other vegetables of choice

Preparation

First get your frying pan or griddle warming up.

Dice or grate the potato(es) and rinse them off in cold water. If starting from already cooked potato skip the rinsing. Grated potato cooks really fast, so it is easy to start from raw.

Dice up any other vegetables you wish to include.

I like to start with browning the onion in butter on med to high heat. Lower heat to med and add the potato. Add other vegetables a minute or so later. If you like zucchini, add it in just before serving to warm it up.

Salt and pepper to tast. Cooked when the potatoes are soft.

Enjoy!
