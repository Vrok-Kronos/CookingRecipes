The Breakfast Wrap

Wrap your breakfast options in a tortilla for a delicious take on the classic.

Ingredients

  • Wraps
  • Bacon!
  • Scrambled Eggs
  • Hashbrowns
  • Grated Cheese
  • Salad dressing of choice

Optional Ingredients

  • Anything else you think should go in a breakfast wrap

Preparation

Cook the bacon, eggs, and hashbrowns. Grate the cheese... Warm up the wraps.

Add everything to the center of the wrap, roll and eat.

Enjoy!
