Mocha Pudding

Its So Chocolaty!

Ingredients (Batter)

  • 1/3 Cup of shortening
  • 2/3 Cup of white sugar
  • 1 Cup of flour
  • 1 & 1/2 Teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 Teaspoons vanilla
  • 1/3 Cup of milk

Ingredients (Sauce)

  • 1/4 Cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/2 Cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 Cup of brown sugar
  • 2 Cups of coffee

Preparation

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees (180 Celsius).

Mix the shortening, sugar, vanilla, and egg(s) together thoroughly in a large bowl. Mix the flour, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl.

Add the dry ingredients to the batter and stir in thoroughly.

Disolve the cocoa powder and sugars into the coffee.

Pour sauce over the batter.

Enjoy!
