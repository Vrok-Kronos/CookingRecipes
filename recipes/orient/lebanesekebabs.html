Savory Lebanese Kebabs

A little something to tickle the tongue.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) of ground beef or pork
  • 1/2 of a medium onion grated and dried
  • 2 Tablespoons (30g) of finely chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 Tablespoons (30g) of finely chopped parsley
  • 2 Tablespoons (30g) of Aleppo pepper
    Or 1/4 Teaspoon (1.25g) of dried pepper flakes
  • 3/4 Teaspoon (3.75g) of finely chopped dried mint
  • 1/2 Teaspoon (2.5g) of ground coriander
  • 1/2 Teaspoon (2.5g) of ground cumin
  • 1/4 Teaspoon (1.25g) of ground allspice
  • 1/4 Teaspoon (1.25g) of ground cinnamon

Optional Ingredients

  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Lebanese Yogurt (Labneh) dipping sauce

Preparation

Mix all of the ingredients together. For the traditional method, grab a handful of the mixture and form a ball. Insert a skewer through the middle and form a kebab along the length of the skewer. It should be roughly 1 inch across (25mm).

Alternatively you can form them into patties to cook as a burger.

For best results cook over a barbeque.

Enjoy!
