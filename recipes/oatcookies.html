Homemade Oatmeal Cookies

Best of the best!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of softened butter
  • 1 Cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 Cups of brown sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Cups of flour
  • 1 Cup of rolled oats
  • 2 Teaspoons of baking powder

Optional Ingredients

  • 50/50 White sugar / Brown sugar for softer cookies
  • 1 & 3/4 Cups of flour and 1/4 Cup of wheat germ
  • 1 Cup of coconut
  • 1 Cup of chocolate chips
  • 1 Cup of raisins
  • 1 tsp each of Baking powder and baking soda
  • If your in the mood... Sprinkles

Preparation

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees (180 Celsius).

Mix the butter, sugar, and egg(s) together thoroughly in a large bowl. Mix your remaining ingredients in a separate bowl.

Add the dry ingredients to the butter batter and stir in thoroughly.

Place on a lighly greased baking sheet in teaspoon sized balls. (These cookies will spread a lot.)

Bake for 10-15 minutes or lightly golden. Keep an eye on them, mine were done in about 9 minutes. (Ovens don't seem to be consistant.)

Makes about 3 dozen cookies.

Enjoy!
