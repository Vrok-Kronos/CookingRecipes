Gregg's Tomato Chicken Soup

Savory Soup that's gluten free to boot!

Ingredients

  • 2 Large chicken breasts
  • 2 Liters of Campbell's Vegetable broth
  • 1 Large can of crushed tomatoes (796ml)
  • 1 Medium can of Aylmer Stewed Tomato with green pepper, celery, & onion (540ml)
  • 3 Stalks of celery
  • 1 Small white onion
  • 1 Red bell pepper
  • 2 Large potatoes
  • 4 Cups (960ml) of frozen peas & carrots
  • 1/4 Cup (60ml) of brown sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons (30ml) of white vinegar
  • 1 Tablespoon (15ml) of Worcestershire sause
  • 2 Teaspoons (10ml) of basil
  • 1 Teaspoon (5ml) of cilantro
  • 1 Teaspoon (5ml) of coriander seeds
  • 1 Teaspoon (5ml) of cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 Teaspoon (2.5ml) of savory
  • 1/2 Teaspoon (2.5ml) of sage
  • Salt to taste

Optional Ingredients

  • Your favorite hot sause for some extra kick

Preparation

First dice up the chicen breasts and vegetables. Brown chicken and onion in a large pot on med-high heat. Add the celery, bell pepper, and potatoes reduce heat to medium.

Next add the canned tomatoes, vegetable broth, and frozen vegetables. Cook for 10 to 15 mins and reduce heat to low.

Mix in remaining sauses and spices. Simmer for about an hour. Stir occasionally.

Enjoy!
