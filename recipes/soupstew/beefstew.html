Gregg's Beef Stew

Hearty Beef Stew.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Lbs(1.5-2kg) of diced beef. (Or ready to go stew beef)
  • 2 Lbs (1kg) of baby carrots
  • 1 Can of tomato paste
  • 1 Large onion
  • 5 Stalks of celery
  • 6 Large potatos
  • 10 Mushrooms
  • 1 Cup (240ml) of red wine
  • 4 Cups (960ml) of frozen peas
  • 6 Cups (1440ml) of hot water
  • 1/2 Cup (120ml) of flour
  • 2 Tablespoons (30ml) of corn starch
  • 1 Tablespoon (15ml) of garlic powder
  • 1 Tablespoon (15ml) of salt
  • 1 Tablespoon (15ml) of black pepper
  • 6 Tablespoons (90ml) of olive oil
  • 8 Teaspoons (40ml) of beef bouillon
  • 1 Teaspoon (5ml) rosemary

Optional Ingredients

  • Gluten free flour and GF bouillon for GF stew. (Try "Better than bouillon" brand.)

Preparation

First Warm olive oil in a large cooking pot on low heat. Combine the flour, garlic powder, salt, and pepper together in a cup.

Then dice the beef into good sized chunks (if you did not buy pre-chopped). Add to pot. Appply the flour mix evenly to the beef so that each chunk gets an even share.

Chop up the onion and add to pot. Cook with a lid until the meat is browned. The juices should look like gravy. Add the red wine and stir in.

Heat up the 6 cups of water and disolve the beef bouillon. Add to pot. Add the can of tomato paste and mix in well. Bring stew base to a low boil.

Dice up the potatos to about the same size as the beef chunks. Cut up the carrots, celery, and mushrooms into appropriate sized pieces. Add to pot along with the rosemary and simmer on low for about an hour.

After the stew has simmered, mix equal parts water and corn starch together in a cup and add to the stew to thicken. It may be a good idea to bring the stew up to a boil before adding the corn starch. Stir it in quickly and thouroghly.

Finally add the peas and cook for another 10 minutes.

Enjoy!
